┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ RISK SCORING                                                                │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Formula:
  risk = (YOLO_conf * 0.55 + cls_prob * 0.40) * class_multiplier

Class Multipliers:
  High-risk (1.5×):   Gun, Bullet, Knife, Baton, HandCuffs, Scissors
  Medium-risk (0.8×): Hammer, Wrench, Pliers
  Low-risk (0.4×):    Powerbank, Lighter, Sprayer

Risk Levels:
  High:   score ≥ 0.70
  Medium: 0.35 ≤ score < 0.70
  Low:    score < 0.35

Note: CLIP (5% weight) dropped intentionally, doesn't affect accuracy
